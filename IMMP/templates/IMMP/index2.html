    How this network works?

    • 1. Invite

      We forward the IMMP presentation for inviting members to the team All members on board can invite Will be using a registration form. Acquiring details about possessed skill sets

    • Evaluate

      Invited members will be interviewed about the depth and experience relating to each skill set Links to past experience and attached documents to be added to profile

    • Add to board

      Once the user is added to the network, he/she can view and opt for projects posted on the board Member can add a description and mention about current interests and availability on profile

    • Assign

      Interested members being assigned to the project This may happen by selection by the project owner Core team and mentors may be consulted

    • Feedback & Rating

      Feedback gained by project owner will be displayed with the member profile This will ase quality control and further recruitment

    Project flow

    • 1

      Idea/Project proposal [Internal or external]

    • 2

      Evaluation and validation by zest core team

    • 3

      Publishing on IMMP board and inviting members

    • 4

      Team formation, assigning duties & setting up milestones

    • 5

      Checking progress and delivery

    • 6

      Feedback
